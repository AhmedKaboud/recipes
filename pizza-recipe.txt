Ingredients:
 - 1 teaspoons sugar
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 1 cup milk
 - 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
 - 1 cup boiling water
 - 2 tomatos
 - 2 meat
 - chease

Directions:
 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking
    pans.
 
 2. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flourand salt. Add milk and water. Mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.
 
 3. add meat, tomatos, and chease to the serface
 
 4. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out
    clean. Cool completely.
